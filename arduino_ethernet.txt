W5100 support SPI modes 0 and 3
SPI frequency: 14 MHz
/SS low to SCLK high time: 21 ns
SCLK high to /SS high time: 21 ns

Set SPI to mode 0 at f_OSC/2
ATmega2560’s SS pin is not wired up. PB4 must be driven as an output (with pull-up).
